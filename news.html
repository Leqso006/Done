2025 Global Tax Reforms – What You Need to Know

New OECD rules, digital-service taxes, and Georgia’s incentives – a full breakdown.

The tax landscape in 2025 is evolving rapidly. The OECD’s Pillar Two framework now mandates a 15% global minimum tax for multinational enterprises with revenue over €750M. This directly impacts cross-border structures.

Key Changes:

  • Digital Services Tax (DST): EU-wide 3–5% on tech giants
  • Georgia Incentives: 0% CIT for IT parks, 5% for free zones
  • Transfer Pricing: Stricter documentation required

Action Step: Review your holding company location. Georgia remains one of the most tax-efficient jurisdictions with full EU market access via DCFTA.
